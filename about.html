ABOUT ME

CASSIDY KOMP

Hi there, I’m Cassidy Komp! I am a front-end developer from and currently based in Milwaukee, Wisconsin.

My Coding Journey

Pre-pandemic I was studying Advertising and Marketing Communications at the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York City, where I graduated with an Associates Degree. I decided to put my schooling on hold while classes were virtual. This interlude gave me the opportunity to rethink my future and the time to pursue coding. I moved back to my home state of Wisconsin and took on the task of creating an online store for a local, family-owned business. Finding an e-commerce platform, learning product photography, building the website. I felt limited by my lack of programming knowledge but intrigued by the endless possibilities. Which led me to the SheCodes workshop. Once I began my coding journey it instantly felt right. I’m excited to continue learning and growing with SheCodes and beyond.
